UZ Tribute Page

Bio

UZ produces dark electronic music rooted in hip-hop and trap. It is dancey like hip-hop but the beats are more dynamic and the 808 bass hits harder. He is from France and, until recently, he has concealed his identity. Upon reveal, he also goes by the alias DJ Troubl', who is a 2 time Disco Mix Club chambion for his turntable scratching.
